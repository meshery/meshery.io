Meshery's Kit

Some guidelines for Meshery have been compiled into a brand kit that can help you better understand and create with the Meshery brand and its assets. For unique use cases on how to utilize our marks in a way not covered here, please contact us and include a visual mockup of intended use.

Meshery

The Meshery brand includes the name, logo, and any word, phrase, image, or other designation that identifies the source or origin of any Layer5 projects. Please DO NOT modify the marks or use them in a confusing way, including suggesting sponsorship or endorsement by Layer5. Also, do not use it in a way that confuses Meshery and Layer5 with any other brand (including your own).
