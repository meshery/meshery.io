The [Shape Builder](https://shapes.meshery.io) extension is one of Meshery's most creative tools — and we are putting out a call for help to move it forward. Whether you are looking to make your first open source contribution or ready to step into a maintainer role, this is a great opportunity to own a meaningful piece of the Meshery ecosystem.

## What is Shape Builder?

[Shape Builder](https://github.com/meshery-extensions/shape-builder) is a Meshery extension that lets anyone visually design custom polygon shapes for use with Kanvas components. Rather than hand-coding coordinate matrices, you draw your shape interactively and the tool outputs the polygon notation that Meshery components understand. It is a great entry point for contributors who want to extend how infrastructure is visualized in Kanvas.

## Where Does It Stand Today?

The project has a solid foundation — roughly 30 contributors and a live deployment. The codebase is primarily JavaScript and CSS, making it very approachable for frontend contributors.

## Where We Need Help

The extension needs one or two volunteers to step up as contributors, remain consistently engaged as champions of the project, and actively steward the project on their way to becoming maintainers. Specifically, we are looking for help with:

- Reviewing and merging (or closing) the backlog of open pull requests
- Triaging and prioritizing open issues
- Helping drive the next feature iteration

### Key Areas of Need

1. **Documentation and guided workflows** — Explaining to users how to put the output of the tool to use: how to copy the code of their newly designed shape, package it, and import it into Meshery.

2. **Tooling and process improvements** — Creating tooling and process for making that workflow smooth — taking custom output and enhancing the components in a user's custom models. If done well, the Shape Builder could grow into a full-blown Meshery component builder.

## How to Get Involved

If you are interested in owning a focused, standalone piece of the Meshery ecosystem, this is your chance. The scope is clear, the stack is frontend-friendly, and the impact is visible every time a user defines a custom component shape in Kanvas.

Reply on the [discussion forum post](https://discuss.meshery.io/t/shape-builder-extension-seeking-contributors-to-become-maintainers/7737) or drop a comment in the [repository](https://github.com/meshery-extensions/shape-builder). Review the [open issues](https://github.com/meshery-extensions/shape-builder/issues) and away you go!

We look forward to welcoming new contributors and future maintainers to the Shape Builder team.
